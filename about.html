The Beginning

College Charter was founded in 2020 by a group of college admissions experts and successful alumni who recognized a gap in the market. Too many bright students were struggling with essay writing and college selection simply because they lacked access to expert guidance.

We decided to change that by building a platform that brings together the best resources, tools, and expertise to help every student succeed.
